Important Guiding Points:

Do's

  • Choose a health insurance plan that suits your needs and budget.
  • Read the policy documents carefully before signing up and ask for clarification if you have any doubts.
  • Renew your policy on time to avoid any policy lapses.
  • Keep your insurer updated about any changes in your personal or medical information.
  • Keep a copy of your health insurance policy and other relevant documents in a safe place.

Dont's

  • Don't hide any pre-existing medical conditions from your insurer as it may lead to claim rejection.
  • Don't delay seeking medical attention as it may impact the coverage of your insurance policy.
  • Don't make fraudulent claims or provide false information to your insurer.
  • Don't assume that all medical treatments are covered under your policy. Check the terms and conditions carefully.
  • Don't ignore the exclusions and limitations of your policy as they may impact your coverage.
